## VISUAL NOTES

### Color Palette
- **Ryan's world:** Warm oranges, soft yellows (cozy but cluttered)
- **Mancave:** Green LED glow, blue screen light
- **Klooster night:** Dark blues, silver moonlight
- **Threat elements:** Red accents (warning text, USB tape)

### Animation Style (South Park Reference)
- Simple cutout animation
- Limited facial expressions (but very expressive)
- Minimal movement, character pops between poses
- Walking = bobbing up and down
- Objects snap into place rather than smooth transitions

### Sound Design
- **Mancave ambient:** Electronic hums, fan noise, occasional beep
- **Outdoor:** Dutch countryside - birds, distant boats, wind
- **Tension:** Low synth drone, heartbeat
- **Tech moments:** Retro computer sounds (beeps, dial-up callbacks)
