# Scene: Home (Kitchen)

## Overview
The starting scene of CyberQuest. Ryan Weylant's white farmhouse with red roof in Compascuum, Netherlands. The scene shows the kitchen where Ryan makes his espresso.

## Visual Description
- Traditional Dutch farmhouse kitchen
- White walls with wooden accents
- Window overlooking the canal with boats passing by
- Espresso machine on the counter
- Road visible in front
- Small canal visible through window
- Cozy, lived-in atmosphere
